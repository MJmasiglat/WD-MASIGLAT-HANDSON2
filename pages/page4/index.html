Darius, The Hand of Noxus

Darius is the epitome of strength and brutality within the Noxian military. As a leader of the Trifarian Legion, he brings justice to those deemed enemies of Noxus, showing no mercy and wielding his axe with unparalleled skill.

Skills:

  • Decimate: Darius swings his axe in a wide circle, dealing damage and healing himself based on the number of enemies hit.
  • Crippling Strike: Darius's next attack deals increased damage and slows the target.
  • Apprehend: Darius pulls in enemies with his axe, slowing them and reducing their armor.
  • Noxian Guillotine: Darius leaps to an enemy champion and executes them, dealing damage based on their missing health. This ability can be re-cast if it results in a kill.
