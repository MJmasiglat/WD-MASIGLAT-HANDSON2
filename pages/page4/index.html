Darius

The Hand of Noxus

Decimate

Darius swings his axe in a wide circle, dealing damage and healing himself based on the number of enemies hit.

Crippling Strike

Darius's next attack deals increased damage and slows the target.

Apprehend

Darius pulls in enemies with his axe, slowing them and reducing their armor.

Noxian Guillotine

Darius leaps to an enemy champion and executes them, dealing damage based on their missing health. This ability can be re-cast if it results in a kill.
