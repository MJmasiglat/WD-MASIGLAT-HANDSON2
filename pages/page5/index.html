Janna, The Storm's Fury

Janna is a powerful elemental mage who uses the power of wind to protect her allies and harass her enemies. She embodies the spirit of the air, bringing both protection and destruction wherever she goes.

Skills:

  • Howling Gale: Janna summons a mighty whirlwind that knocks up enemies in its path.
  • Zephyr: Janna summons an air elemental to deal damage and slow an enemy, while passively increasing her movement speed.
  • Eye Of The Storm: Janna shields an ally, absorbing damage and increasing their attack damage.
  • Monsoon: Janna releases a powerful storm that heals allies and knocks back enemies.
