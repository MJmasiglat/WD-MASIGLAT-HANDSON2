Teemo, The Swift Scout

Teemo is a yordle with an unbreakable sense of morality and determination. Despite his small stature, Teemo never hesitates to embark on dangerous missions, relying on his wits and unique abilities to outmaneuver his enemies.

Skills:

  • Blinding Dart: Teemo shoots a dart at an enemy, blinding them and causing them to miss their attacks.
  • Move Quick: Teemo passively gains increased movement speed and can activate this ability to gain an even greater speed boost for a short time.
  • Toxic Shot: Teemo's basic attacks poison the target, dealing damage over time.
  • Noxious Trap: Teemo places an explosive trap that detonates when an enemy steps on it, poisoning and slowing all nearby enemies.
