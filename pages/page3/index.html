Blitzcrank, The Great Steam Golem

Blitzcrank is a powerful, steam-driven automaton from Zaun, designed for heavy labor. His massive frame and incredible strength make him a formidable force on the battlefield, capable of pulling enemies into his clutches and disrupting their formations.

Skills:

  • Rocket Grab: Blitzcrank fires his right hand to grab an enemy, dealing damage and pulling them towards him.
  • Overdrive: Blitzcrank gains a burst of speed, increasing his movement and attack speed for a short duration.
  • Power Fist: Blitzcrank's next attack is empowered, dealing double damage and knocking the target into the air.
  • Static Field: Blitzcrank passively marks enemies he attacks, periodically discharging electricity to damage them. When activated, he unleashes a powerful electrical field that damages all nearby enemies and silences them temporarily.
