Garen, The Might of Demacia

Garen is a proud and noble warrior, dedicated to the defense of Demacia and its ideals. He wields his broadsword with honor and courage, fighting to protect his homeland and its people.

Skills:

  • Decisive Strike: Garen gains a burst of speed, breaking free of all slows affecting him and his next attack deals bonus damage and silences his target.
  • Courage: Garen passively increases his armor and magic resist. He can also activate this ability to gain a shield and reduce incoming damage for a short period.
  • Judgment: Garen rapidly spins around with his sword, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies.
  • Demacian Justice: Garen calls upon the might of Demacia to attempt to execute an enemy champion, dealing damage based on their missing health.
